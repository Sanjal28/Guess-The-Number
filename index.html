Rules and Regulations for "Guess The Number From 1 To 10" Game

Objective:

The player must guess a randomly generated number between 1 and 10.

Gameplay:

Enter a number in the input field and click the "Check" button to submit your guess. A new random number is generated each time you click "Check."

Scoring:

The game starts with a score of 10. For each incorrect guess, 1 point is deducted from the score.

Winning:

If you guess the number correctly, you win the round. A message will confirm your correct guess, and the generated number will be displayed.

Input Restrictions:

Only numbers between 1 and 10 are valid inputs. If you enter a number greater than 10, you will receive a warning, and your score will decrease.

Game End:

The game ends when the score reaches 0. The player can refresh the page to play again.

Result Display:

After each guess, the game will display whether your guess was correct or incorrect and show the randomly generated number for that round.
